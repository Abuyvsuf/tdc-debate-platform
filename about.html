THE PEOPLE BEHIND THE CIRCLE

Origin Story

Started in Nairobi, built for Kenya's next generation.

TDC began as a small after-school forum where students debated civic issues in a safe, structured space. As participation grew, we expanded into county-level circuits, mentorship labs, and national showcases that connect schools across Kenya.

Mission

Building an informed citizenry one student at a time through debates and conversations.

Tagline
